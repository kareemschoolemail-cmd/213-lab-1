So many apples

In our modern grocery stores, at least in the west, there are so many
apples. The moment you enter the fruit isle of any grocery store, there
are at least three different kinds of apples on sale throughout the year.
Amongst the most common being Gala apples, Granny Smith apples, and Red
delicious apples. Gala apples are descibed as being sweet and mild, being good
for snacking. Red delicious apples are softer than most apples, and are typic
ally less sweet than the other apples mentioned. Granny smiths aren't known
for their sweetness, but rather their powerful sour flavor.
